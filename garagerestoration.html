Frequently Asked Questions: Garage Restoration

How long does a typical garage restoration project take?

Project duration varies greatly depending on the size of the garage, the extent of damage, and the specific repairs needed. Small repairs might take a few days, while comprehensive restorations can take several weeks to months. We provide a detailed timeline with our proposal after a thorough assessment.

What are the common signs my garage needs restoration?

Look for cracking or spalling concrete, exposed rebar (rusting steel), water leaks or stains, pooling water, or heaving/uneven floor slabs. Any visible deterioration warrants a professional inspection.

Can my garage remain operational during restoration?

Whenever possible, we implement phased work plans to keep sections of the garage open and minimize disruption to tenants and visitors. We'll discuss specific logistical needs during the planning phase.
